## Research Context

The objective is not only better self-supervised representation quality, but a better structural prior for the prediction operator itself. In this framing, isotropy is treated as a special case, not a default geometric truth.

## Practical Interpretation

HamJEPA treats the predictor as a structure-preserving map in phase space. This gives a route to better stability diagnostics (energy drift, reversibility, and volume preservation) while maintaining competitive learning dynamics.

## Technical Notes

- The predictor is designed to preserve geometric structure rather than merely regularize covariance.
- Diagnostics such as symplecticity defect, reversibility, and drift are treated as model health signals.
