## Problem

HamJEPA changes the predictor itself: it rolls a phase-space state forward with a symplectic map. In this framing, isotropy is treated as a special case, not a default geometric truth.

## What changes

HamJEPA treats the predictor as a structure-preserving map in phase space. This gives a route to better stability diagnostics (energy drift, reversibility, and volume preservation) while maintaining competitive learning dynamics.

## Notes

- The predictor transports a phase-space state instead of only regularizing covariance.
- Energy drift, reversibility, and symplecticity defect are checked directly.
