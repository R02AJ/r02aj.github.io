Selected evidence

HamJEPA

A JEPA variant that predicts between views by rolling a q,p state through a symplectic Hamiltonian map instead of forcing the latent cloud toward an isotropic Gaussian.

Arbitrage-free option surfaces

Fits forward-discounted call prices with Chebyshev tensors and sparse no-arbitrage operators; local fog handles inconsistent quote patches.

Geometric stochastic modelling

A joint spot–curve–surface model where no-arbitrage and transport structure are built into the state update, not checked after calibration.

Optiver / market making

Quant trading and market-making training focused on options quoting, inventory risk, hedge execution, and latency-aware decisions under live exchange simulation.
